Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 – 2015

“Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep.”

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India’s missile and space programs and was widely known as the Missile Man of India.

Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he rose from humble beginnings through dedication, discipline, and relentless hard work. His journey from a small-town boy to the President of India continues to inspire millions.

More than a scientist, he was a teacher at heart. He believed that the youth of India were the true strength of the nation and constantly encouraged students to dream big and work hard to achieve those dreams.
